Our Story

A passion for authentic Italian cuisine

Founded in 1995 by the Rossi family, Bella Vista has been serving authentic Italian cuisine to Vancouver for over 25 years. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, bringing the true taste of Italy to your table.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients, from San Marzano tomatoes to aged Parmigiano-Reggiano, ensuring every dish captures the essence of traditional Italian cooking.

- Chef Marco Rossi
