Frequently asked questions

Have a question not listed? Reach out and we’ll respond within one business day.

How long does a typical project take?
Most websites launch within 2-5 weeks depending on page count, approvals, and readiness of content assets.
What’s included in your pricing packages?
Every tier covers strategy sessions, custom design, responsive development, quality assurance, and launch support.
Do you handle copywriting and imagery?
We refine existing copy, provide structure for new sections, and offer curated stock imagery when brand assets are limited.
How many revision rounds are included?
Each phase includes structured feedback windows—typically three rounds during design and one during development.
Can you migrate our current site?
Yes, we audit existing content, map redirects, and migrate assets to preserve SEO equity and historical data.
Do you provide hosting?
We recommend managed hosting partners and can set up, maintain, and monitor your chosen platform as part of maintenance.
How is SEO handled?
We deliver on-page optimization, technical improvements, and content briefs aligned to your service area keywords.
Do you work with in-house marketing teams?
Absolutely—we plug into internal workflows, provide training, and document systems so your team can iterate confidently.
What if we need ongoing updates?
Our maintenance retainer covers scheduled updates, new pages, and proactive improvements each month.
