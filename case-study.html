Coastal Clinic

Doubling patient bookings with a streamlined experience and clear value messaging.

Industry: Healthcare Timeline: 10 weeks Services: Design · Build · SEO

Overview

Modernizing patient journeys

Coastal Clinic needed to improve online visibility, simplify appointment scheduling, and elevate trust for new and returning patients. We delivered a patient-first site with streamlined paths to book, read up on practitioners, and access resources.

Project snapshot

Comprehensive redesign mapped around patient questions and service discovery.

Team

Strategy lead, product designer, Webflow developer, SEO analyst.

Platform

Responsive Webflow build with Zapier workflows for intake forms.

Goals

Objectives set at kickoff

We captured business metrics before design, ensuring success metrics were clear.

Increase bookings

Lift online appointment requests by 40% within three months.

Clarify services

Improve how specialties and practitioners are introduced.

Boost visibility

Reach first-page rankings for key local treatment keywords.

Process

How we delivered

A four-phase sprint aligned stakeholders and ensured measurable improvements.

Audit

Analyzed analytics, top queries, and recorded patient calls for insight.

Experience

Produced wireframes with improved navigation and clear booking triage.

Build

Developed modular CMS collections and integrated intake automations.

Optimize

Deployed launch checklist, heatmap tracking, and reporting dashboards.

Results

Measurable wins

Data pulled from 90 days post-launch compared to the previous quarter.

+72% bookings

Appointment completions climbed after simplifying scheduling.

+55% organic traffic

Service pages now capture top-three positions for 8 new keywords.

1.8s faster

Performance optimization reduced homepage load times significantly.
